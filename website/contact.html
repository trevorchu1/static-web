About Us

Welcome to Skate Haven! We are passionate about skateboarding and dedicated to providing the best products and services for all skaters, from beginners to pros.

Founded in 2020, Skate Haven was established to create a community hub for skateboard enthusiasts. Our team consists of experienced skaters who understand the needs of our customers.

We offer a wide range of skateboards, accessories, and apparel, ensuring that you have everything you need to hit the streets or the skatepark. Join us for workshops, events, and skate competitions that bring our community together!

Open 11-9 Tuesday through Sunday, visit our board shop and indoor skatepark! Upcoming events are posted outside by the door.
